Cookie durations play a crucial role in affiliate marketing, as they determine how long an affiliate can earn commission from a referred customer. When a customer clicks on an affiliate’s referral link and makes a purchase or takes a desired action, a cookie is placed in their browser. This cookie contains information that identifies the affiliate as the source of the referral. The duration of this cookie determines the length of time during which the affiliate can earn a commission on any subsequent purchases made by that customer.

Each affiliate program sets its own cookie duration, which can vary widely. Some programs offer short cookie durations, typically ranging from a few hours to a few days, while others offer longer durations that can extend to several months. It is important for affiliates to consider the cookie duration when choosing which programs to promote, as a longer duration gives them a greater opportunity to earn commission from repeat purchases made by referred customers.
