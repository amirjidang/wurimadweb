When you are famous, there are certain things that come with it. One of those things is that the world is constantly going to be up in your business. It is just part of the job and the reality is that some people love the limelight more than others.

Hollywood is filled with all different kinds of celebrities. What we tend to forget is that celebrities are pretty much regular people who have more money and who are more popular than the public.

Like all humans, everyone is different. Some celebrities love the attention that comes with fame; they love the paparazzi, having their photos in the tabloids and the internet flooding with stories about them. On the other hand, there are some celebrities who love being under the radar; that you only hear about when they are promoting a new movie and whom you rarely see making the cover of tabloids.

We thought it would be fun to compare celebrities that love the limelight versus those celebrities that try their hardest to fly under the radar. From A-list movie stars to C-list reality stars, this list has all kinds of celebrities, proving that some people are simply cooler than others.

Mila Kunis: Low-Key

Mila Kunis has been in the world of show business for most of her life. The actress who got her big break on the show That '70s Show has had quite an impressive career. From appearing in films like Bad Moms, Black Swan, Forgetting Sarah Marshall and Friends with Benefits it is cool that with all her fame, she still tries to fly under the radar.

Kunis is not one of those celebrities that craves paparazzi. She tries to keep her life as private as possible and even though she is married to another A-list celebrity, they have managed to keep their lives as private as they can.

Kunis has gone on record stating “That is the biggest form of bullying ever, the paparazzi. Printing lies, making accusations, it's just bullying,” which can explain why she avoids them at all costs.

Amber Rose: Loves Attention

Amber Rose is one of those people with no business being famous. The “actress” is one of the people on the planet who craves the most attention.

Rose is known for dating as many rappers as she can and she prides herself on the fact that she does so. The fact that she dates these rappers is the only link to fame that she has and Rose is not going to let go of that fame easily.

Amber Rose once stated “Like every poor person, I used to dream about winning the lottery. I didn't just get money, though. I got fame. And I got fame before I got money, and it was scary” which is one of the most honest things she has ever said.

We do not hate Amber Rose, we just do not know how someone can need so much attention from the masses.

Jennifer Garner: Low-Key

Jennifer Garner is one of the most adorable and underrated celebrities out there. The mother of three who was once married to superstar Ben Affleck tries to keep their lives and their children live as normal as possible.

Although Jennifer Garner is a huge superstar, her main job and priority are being a mother, and her actions show exactly that.

We live in a celebrity-obsessed culture and Garner agrees with us “Why does everyone think they need to be a star? It's ridiculous. The celebrity culture is so silly, and the fact that people grow up thinking that it's something to aspire to just seems wrong. I don't mean to bash my life. I love my life; I just think it's not the only way to go” stated the star in a previous interview.

Heidi Montag: Loves Attention

There are not many people in the world that love attention as much as Heidi Montag. The formers reality star is one of those people who pushed their fifteen minutes of fame as far as it could go. Her idea of fame is slightly warped and she and husband Spencer Pratt do almost anything for a spread in a magazine.

Montag who had undergone a tremendous amount of cosmetic surgeries admits that she may have gone a little too far “I got too caught up in Hollywood, being so into myself and my image” said the former reality star.

Now, Montag is a new, and of course, the internet almost immediately knew about it. In life, you just need to accept that some things never chance and Heidi Montag's need for attention is one of them.

Blake Lively: Low-Key

Blake Lively is beautiful, talented and super famous. However, even with all the fame she and husband Ryan Reynolds share, they try and keep their lives as low key as possible.

We believe that the best Hollywood couples are those that are the most low key, which is exactly why we love Blake Lively and her husband so much.

Lively, who is known for her roles in Gossip Girl, Green Lantern, and Savages is one of the world's classiest and most poised ladies. Lively once stated “People gossip. People are insecure, so they talk about other people so that they won't be talked about. They point out flaws in other people to make them feel good about themselves. I think at any age or any social class, that's present.”

We love that she is a kick-ass mother and wife and the little bit that she has shown us of her family is enough to fall in love with them!

Farah Abraham: Loves Attention

Farah Abraham was a teen mom who profited off her pregnancy by being on the reality show about pregnant sixteen-year-olds. Since then she has found any way of making a dollar including starring in a naughty film.

Abraham has done a lot of things for attention and there is no denying that this former reality star loves the fame that came with being on television. She has defended her parenting skills on countless occasions and is delusional when it comes to what being a mother is all about.

Abraham has been questioned about her parenting skills on numerous occasions. Perhaps she should focus less on being famous and more on being a good mother.

Sia: Low-Key

Sia is so low key that when she started getting super famous she decided she had to hide her face.

Sia is a very talented recording artist who attempted to separate her image from her craft, which she did with success. The singer is trying to prove to the world and her fans that despite her fame, she is still doing this for the music.

Sia went on record stating “I don't care what people think of me unless they think I'm mean or something, but I don't care if they think I'm like someone else because I know I'm not - I'm a total weirdo. I'm not selling a dream; I'm not selling fame like it is some sort of fantastic thing. I'm just trying to sell music and get on with my real life” and we commend her for living her life that way.

The world needs more celebrities like Sia – talented and not in it for the attention.

Floyd Mayweather: Loves Attention

When it comes to loving attention there are not many people who can say they love it as much as this guy.

Floyd Mayweather is one of the best boxers of our generation, however, he is also one of the cockiest and least low key men to ever walk this earth.

Floyd is one of those people who goes on record and states “Most people should be talking about how Floyd Mayweather is a great undefeated future Hall of Famer that's his own promoter and that works extremely hard to get to where he's at. Instead, all you hear is hate and jealous remarks from critics who criticize me and, you know, most of the time, the people that criticize me can't do what I can do.”

There is no denying how super talented he is, there is also no denying how much he loves attention, which is why he made this list.

Kristen Bell: Low-Key

Kristen Bell is one of the most adorable and lovable actresses in Hollywood. She and husband Dax Shepard have two beautiful children and together they try to give them the most normal life possible.

Bell is one of those actors who is in it for the craft, not the fame and has even said “I'm not in the business of becoming famous. And that's the advice I give to younger aspiring actors. Work onstage and do the little roles. In the end, it's not important to be seen. It's important to do. There's a lot of disappointment in this business, but my family keeps me grounded.”

The world needs more low key ladies like Bell, making her one of the coolest women around Hollywood.

Kardashian Family: Loves Attention

When you think of celebrities that crave attention, there is no denying that The Kardashian family was right up there. The Kardashians are pretty much famous for being famous and they have done everything in their power to continue growing that fame.

Kris Jenner, the matriarch of the family, has gone on record stating, “I'm strong enough and have a pretty thick skin, but when people go after my kids, I just hit block-delete, block-delete. It's my mantra.” With her mama bear mentality in full check, it is no wonder why everything that her kids do ends up being something that she can monetize on.

Love them or hate them, the Kardashians have used their fame to their benefit and each of them is continuing to grow both their individual empires and their families empire.

Anna Kendrick: Low-Key

Anna Kendrick is one of those celebrities that seem to be “just like us.” She is one of those girls that you feel you would be friends with if ever you met her at a bar or something.

Kendrick is one of those people who still does not believe they are famous “I rarely get recognized. It's always a shock when someone notices me. I always think they must be confusing me with someone else,” which is adorable.

Kendrick is best known for her roles in Pitch Perfect, The Accountant and Mike and Dave Need Wedding Dates, all of which demonstrate how talented and adorable she is.

We love how down to earth and low key Anna Kendrick is and we wish more girls would look up to her instead of some other women on this list.

Blac Chyna: Loves Attention

There was no one in 2017 more desperate for fame and attention than Blac Chyna.

Chyna, who is the mother of Rob Kardashian's daughter, is one of the most fame-hungry females the world has ever seen. The stripper turned rappers baby mama, turned reality star turned Kardashian baby mama is doing pretty well for herself. She managed to gain enough popularity and fortune to live a life filled with lavish belongings.

Although Chyna has gone on record saying “Despite my high profile on social media, I'm a private person” this year has proven that is a lie. We hope 2018 is the year that we stop hearing the name, Blac Chyna. It is time for someone else to have her fifteen minutes of fame because Chyna's clock has officially run out.

Jessica Alba: Low-Key

Jessica Alba is without a doubt one of the most stunning women on the planet. Not only is she fiercely beautiful but she is also talented and down to earth. The actress, despite all her fame, is one of the most low key people in Hollywood and tries to separate her job from the fame that comes with it.

Alba who once said You have to be the best of whatever you are, but successful, cool actresses come in all shapes and sizes” is one of the classiest ladies around and her low key profile displays how real of a person she is. The mother of two is a woman that is easily admired and she is truly awesome.

Cast Of Vanderpump Rules: Loves Attention

The cast of the reality show Vanderpump Rules really got lucky for a group of waiters and bartenders. The show which is about the cast of Lisa Vanderpump's restaurant has gained a lot of popularity over the years. Lisa Vanderpump who is one of the housewives of Beverly Hills said in a previous interview that she does not watch television besides her shows “I don't watch any television, hardly ever because I'm so busy. I always obviously watch my shows because I blog about it and talk about it, but no, I can watch the news in the morning and that's it.”

The cast is filled with fame-hungry monsters that seem to all be enjoying and monetizing on their momentary fame and we can't really blame them.

All of the cast members are incredibly active on social media so if you do not believe us about their fame hunger, go and check it out for yourselves!

Emma Watson: Low-Key

Emma Watson is one of those celebrities that oozes class and beauty. The actress has been famous since she was a child and has tried to keep her life as normal as possible.

Watson, who is a graduate of Brown University, always put an emphasis on her education, although it was hard for her to fit into a “normal” life.

Watson opened up in an interview, stating, “I threw my 20th birthday party at Brown, and I didn't even have to say to anyone not to put pictures on Facebook. Not a single picture went up. That was when I knew I'd found a solid group of friends, and I felt like I belonged.” This is a perfect example of how low-key and down to earth Watson truly is.

Jonathan Cheban: Loves Attention

Jonathan Cheban is known to many as the “food god” and is one of those people who is famous for being friends with famous people. The reality star and entrepreneur is Kim Kardashians best friend which is why he has gotten so popular.

Cheban loves attention and makes it obvious by the posts on social media as well as the places he chooses to spend his time at. This attention seeker may have no right being famous but he does know a lot about eating out and boasting to the world about his delicious meals which are almost as obnoxious as who he is as a person.

Regardless of this, there is something you can't help but love about Cheban which is why he probably still has so many people interested in his life.
